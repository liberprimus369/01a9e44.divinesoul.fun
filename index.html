What is a Divine Soul?​​

It is a person who was selected by Saint Valentine the Saint of love to be with you, this person has been connected to you since the day of your birth, making one body belong to the other.

Just those bodies knowing that, they become one.

That has nothing to do with soulmate, because soulmate isn't related to marriage, so it can be anyone you have a good relationship with, like your father, your mother, or even your child.

Different from the divine soul, which was appointed to be with you, with an extremely deep connection, as never seen before. The divine soul is the right person for you and will be revealed by Master Rasmus, through a gift received from Saint Valentine, the Saint of Love who had the function of uniting couples, and on February 14th, his day is celebrated, Valentine's Day.
